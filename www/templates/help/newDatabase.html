Teams and Databases
To collaborate with other Shuri users you’ll need to create a shared Database by assigning it an Editing Team. Then, every member of that Team will have access to view and edit its content.

To start with creating a Database first, simply enter the Database’s name and select A New Team. Give your Team a name and select Add Database. Now your new Team and Database will already be synced.

Build out your Team

To add teammates, click on your Team’s name from the Shuri homepage. Start typing a teammate’s name into the People tab and find your desired entry listed in the drop-down menu. Make sure that all of your teammates have made Shuri accounts before taking this step.

If you’re not collaborating with anyone at this time, you can simply make a Database without a Team. Teams can be changed or assigned later, just click on the Edit button in your Database’s tab on the homepage and find Editing Team in the Sharing section.
