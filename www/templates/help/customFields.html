Custom Fields
Custom field definition : person, org and Touch edit pages list the fields that Shuri offers. Most of these are for storing basic contact information such as: email and phone numbers, etc… But, if you’re tracking a different kind of data, you can customize your own field to display on the EDIT and VIEW pages of a profile.

From the new Custom Field page:
  • Create your new field’s name
  • Select whether you’d like for this field to apply to People, Organizations, and/or Touches.
  • Then, specify which type of field you’d like to create. Shuri offers the capability for text fields or ratings like: 0-100, 5 stars and yes/no/maybe.
  • Lastly, choose if you’d like this field to live only inside of the selected Database or to span all of your Databases, then click Save. (If you want to use this field for contacts in Shuri’s Analyst Relations Database, select for the field to span all Databases)

The next time you navigate to a contact’s EDIT page, your custom field will appear.
