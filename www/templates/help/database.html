Customizing in Shuri

Track your own data with Custom Fields, Tags and Touch Types

The best way to keep your contacts organized is with custom fields, tags and Touch types.

To access these features, click on Customize from a Database’s edit page.

What are they?

  • Custom fields: person, org and Touch edit pages list the fields that Shuri offers. Most of these are for storing basic contact information such as: email and phone numbers, etc… But, if you’re tracking a different kind of data, you can customize your own field to display on the EDIT and VIEW pages of a profile.
  • Tags: are like labels that offer a way to track trends and categories across many items and then quickly find what you’re looking for in queries. To learn more about tags, see TAGS & QUERYING.
  • Touch type: dictates what kind of interaction you’re tracking and each Touch type has a different set of fields to fit the interaction accordingly.
For more info, see CUSTOM FIELDS, TAG SETS, or TOUCH TYPES
