Imports
Importing your Twitter contacts
You might first be asked to log into Twitter if you haven’t done so already. Then, create a Group and choose a Database for the import. Next, you’ll see a list of those that you follow on Twitter.

Select individually or toggle at the top of the page to select all, then click Import. Before you know it, you’ll be back on Shuri’s homepage with your contacts stored in your chosen Database and Group.

To fill in more information about these contacts, see FLESH OUT YOUR DATA.
