Teams and Databases
To collaborate with other Shuri users you’ll need to create a shared Database by assigning it an Editing Team. Then, every member of that Team will have access to view and edit its content. You can also share the Database with a Team to get view-only permissions.

To start with creating a Team first, simply enter in a name and toggle on for Create a Team Database.

This will make a new Database with your Team assigned to it. Teammates will automatically see both the Team and Database on their homepages after logging in, and can start working with them.

Build out your Team

To add teammates, click on your Team’s name from the Shuri homepage. Start typing a teammate’s name into the People tab and find your desired entry listed in the drop-down menu. Make sure that all of your teammates have made Shuri accounts before taking this step.
