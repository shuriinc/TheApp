Shuri’s Analyst Relations Database

Changes in the analyst community can be tough to track. Recognizing these challenges, Shuri has developed a deep database of over 7,000 industry analysts and more than 300 organizations worldwide.

This database is designed with an emphasis on the key people you need to know and their essential contact information.

All analysts and organizations are tagged according to their:
  • Areas of Coverage, with 31 tags including: Internet of Things, Digital Marketing, Servers & Storage, Supply Chain, and more.
  • Industry, with 17 tags including: Healthcare, Media & Entertainment, Energy & Utilities, Financial Services, and more.
  • Region
  • Role we’ve separated out : analysts, consultants, researchers, and/or executives from each other.

A living Database

Shuri employs a group of dedicated internet researchers who update the AR Database daily, turning it over completely every 90 days. This can be useful when tracking analysts who are constantly moving from one organization to another.

We also understand that collaboration is key to making the best Database possible and invite you to edit the People and Organizations in the Database with any relevant information. All of your edits will be forwarded to the Shuri Subscriptions Team for approval.
