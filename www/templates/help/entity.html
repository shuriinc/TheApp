Editing Basics

On every Person, Organization and Touch profile there is an EDIT page and a VIEW page.

The EDIT page is your chance to add information about this item. All info added here will display on the item’s VIEW page.

How to navigate to the edit page for any item:
  • By clicking on the pencil at the top left corner of it’s view page.
  • By clicking the 3 lines on the righthand side of an item’s name from Shuri’s homepage and selecting Edit.

Once on the EDIT page you’ll see Shuri’s pre-populated fields (we’ve got the basics covered like: Email, Business Phone, and social media URL fields for LinkedIn, Facebook and Twitter, among others).

Know More

To gather more information about your contacts from the internet, find the fields with a Search link.

Clicking Search will open a new search on your web browser, so you can find the information you’re looking for.

Once you’re on the page you’d like to capture, click Done at the bottom of the page for iOS and the X in the top right corner of the page for Android.

Next, you’ll be taken back to the edit page on Shuri, and the URL of that webpage will be entered into the appropriate field. (For app.shuri.com users, copy and paste the link manually)

Finalizing Edits

Once you’ve entered as much info as you’d like, click Save. Back on the profile’s view page, those main contact points will appear as a link in the contact bar above. Other fields will display in the Details section and assorted tabs below.

Subscribers to Shuri’s Analyst Relations Database can make edits to an analyst’s profile page, and each edit will be forwarded to our Shuri Subscriptions Team for review.

Where Your Data Lives

To change which Database an item lives in, scroll to the bottom of its EDIT page, and open the Sharing section. There, you'll find a selection for Databases, where you can choose from a drop-down menu of those available to you.
