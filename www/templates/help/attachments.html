Attachments
Storing and Sharing Files
With Shuri, attach files to any People, Organizations or Touches. Share these files with your teammates in a shared Database and cut down on sending email attachments back and forth.

To attach files on a device, you’ll need to have a file-sharing app like Google Drive or Dropbox already installed. On a device you’ll also have access to attach photos, videos or audio recordings.

If you’re on a desktop, you’ll have access to attach all files stored on your computer.
