Spreadsheet Import
Importing a spreadsheet from your device or cloud to Shuri is easy.

If your spreadsheet contains people, organizations, and/or touches (aka interactions, activities, etc.) then it can be imported.

Every person, org, and/or touch will be imported into a database that you choose. The database is where all the imported items will be stored and it determines who will be able to see and modify the things you import.

In addition, everything gets placed into a new Private Group, created to segment everything imported in case you need an import "do over."

Here's the process:
  1. Upload your spreadsheet from your device or cloud
  2. Choose the database that will house the imported data. The database should already exist.
  3. Choose the name for your new Private Group.
  4. For each column in your spreadsheet, identify what information it contains. We provide a list for you to choose from or you may create your own information field.
  5. Press "Import Now."
  6. In case of import problems there is an "UNDO": the Private Group created during the import may be completely deleted, which removes all the imported data from the database, letting you start over.
Once imported, all of the people, organizations, and touches that were in your spreadsheet will be in both the new Private Group and the Database you chose. Enjoy the new 360-degree view of your relationships!
